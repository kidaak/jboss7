Welcome to the Board of Behavioral Health Examiners online credit card payment service. We are pleased to provide this convenient service for professionals who are applying for a new license, renewing an existing license, or are requesting other services.

Please Note

  • Include a copy of your printed or emailed payment receipt with your application.
  • If you are paying your license issuance fee, begin by selecting the Apply For License option on the next page.
  • If you are paying your license renewal fee and it is after your license expiration date but still within 90 days of your expiration date, you will be assessed an additional $100 late fee.
  • You will not be able to process payment of your license renewal fee if it is 90 days past your expiration date as your license has now expired and cannot be renewed.
  • There may be a $3.00 online convenience fee added to your transaction.

We may be contacted at (602) 542-1882 if you have questions or concerns regarding your payment.

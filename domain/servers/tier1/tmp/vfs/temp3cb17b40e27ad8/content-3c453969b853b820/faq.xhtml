Frequently Asked Questions

  • This online payment system is used to pay Office of Accounts Receivable and Collections (OARC) balances only. For Child Support payments and Employer UI Tax payments, please contact those offices for the proper procedures. Note that you should use this online system to make your Unemployment Insurance Benefit Overpayment payment.
  • View Tutorial.
  • View your online E-Statement.
  • We process all credit card transactions immediately for the amount that you authorize, however, your credit card statement may not reflect the transaction for several days. Your credit card transaction will appear with the name of "AZ DEPT OF ECONOMIC SEC".

Note: Any online payment will not be reflected on your online statement for 24 hours. If the delay is longer than 24 hours, you should contact the DES switchboard at (602) 252-0024 or toll free in the state of Arizona at 1-800-236-1475 for more information.
  • Online payments will not be issued as a cash refund. All refunds will be issued directly to your credit card.
  • Since your credit card information is not retained in our records, you may be contacted to provide information necessary to process your refund.
  • To get a refund, please contact the DES switchboard at (602) 252-0024 or toll free in the State of Arizona at 1-800-236-1475 for more information. You can also e-mail us for further information.
  • The card verification number is a security requirement implemented by credit card companies to help reduce fraud for internet, phone or mail transactions. Since the card verification number is listed on your credit card but not stored anywhere, the only way to have the card verification number is to physically have possession of the credit card.
  • The card verification number for American Express is the 4 digits printed (not embossed) on the front of the card above the credit card number on the right.

  • The card verification number for MasterCard, Visa, or Discover is the last 3 digits of the number in the signature area on the back of your card.

Overview

The DES Online Payment System is used to pay accounts due to the Office of Accounts Receivable and Collections (OARC). Payments for Child Support and Employer UI Tax do not use this system.

Payments should be mailed to the following address.
Make your payment payable to the "Arizona Department of Economic Security".

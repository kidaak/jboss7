Thank you for choosing to pay your bill online. You will need your ten-digit account number, including zeros, which is printed on the Monthly Statement of Accounts. For identity you will be asked for your date of birth (month/day). To pay online you will need a Visa, MasterCard, Discover Card, American Express credit card, or a bank debit card displaying the Visa logo.

Note: If you are making COURT ORDERED RESTITUTION or PAYING OFF A JUDGMENT you SHOULD NOT use this online system before contacting OARC at 602-252-0024 for payment instructions.

This online payment system is used to pay Office of Accounts Receivable and Collections (OARC) balances only, including Unemployment Insurance Benefit (UIB) overpayments.

For Child Support payments and Employer UI Tax payments, please contact those offices for the proper procedures.

You may view your Online E-Statement by selecting this link.

The DES Mainframe System is unavailable for payments on Saturdays between 3:00 - 5:00 p.m.
